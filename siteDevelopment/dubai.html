Dubai

The city that never sleeps. Home of fine golden sands and vast Arabian desert. Famous for having the tallest building on the planet – Burj Khalifa with a total height of 830-metre which is almost three times the size of the Eiffel Tower. It’s a city that is built in a concrete jungle of infrastructures and greatest skyline of skyscrapers.

If you love going for a shopping spree, you can’t get enough with the world’s biggest mall – The Dubai Mall has over 12 million square feet in total area, an equivalent to more than 50 soccer fields. A single day won’t be enough to fully explore the area. It houses a huge aquarium and an underwater zoo as well right in the centre of the mall. It can be viewed for free from the mall through the world’s largest viewing area made out of acrylic panel. Getting inside the aquarium will give you an amazing 270 degrees view through the 48-metre long aquarium tunnel.
