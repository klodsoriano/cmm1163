Visit Dubai

Dubai is one of the fastest growing tourist destinations globally with the world’s busiest airport bringing 90 million passengers annually. It is home of more than 400 Guinness World Records and famously known as the title holder of the world’s tallest building – the Burj Khalifa. It is also known as the summer capital destination with its white fine sands and crystal-clear beaches. Also, not to mention the state-of-the-art megastructures with futuristic design concepts are everywhere in the city.

Just recently, Ain Dubai was officially opened to public and is currently the world’s largest and tallest observation wheel. It joins Dubai’s skyline with a height of 820 feet topping Las Vegas’ high roller observation wheel. Also, you can’t miss the newly opened Deep Dive Dubai – the world’s deepest swimming pool with a depth of 60-metre. If you’re up into more adventure and heart- stopping activity, you can go sky diving at Sky Dive Dubai, jumping out off a plane from the top of the world’s most iconic man-made island, Palm Jumeirah or try the world’s longest zip line ride at Jebel Jais with a travel speed of up to 150 km/h at a height of 1,680- metre above sea level on top of Jebel Jais Mountain. Dubai is indeed one of the best get-away and picture-perfect destinations in the world.
